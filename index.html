FAQ's

How do I sell my Farm Equipment?

You can sell your Farm Equipment by listing it on FarmEasy website or FarmEasy mobile applications.

How can I list my Equipment?

Listing on FarmEasy is quite easy. Just click on the button LIST YOUR EQUIPMENT on the top right corner of the website. Choose equipment type and location and click “List Now”. Fill in the details of the equipment and click on the submit button once you complete it.

How do I sell New or used tractor?

You can sell your new or used tractor by listing it on FarmEasy website or FarmEasy mobile applications. Don’t forget to mark it as new or used while creating equipment listing.

Is there an option to buy equipment?

Yes there is an option to buy equipment as well. Some providers are open to selling equipment on FarmEasy with a fair deal.

I don’t have a website, can I still sell on FarmEasy.app?

You don't need a website to start selling on FarmEasy. Once you complete registration, you will have access to your account where you can manage your equipment listing.
